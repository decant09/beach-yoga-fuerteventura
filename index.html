About Us

We are a yoga club based in El Cotillo, Fuerteventura. Our aim is to take you to a place where you can re-connect with nature, become grounded and tap into the special energy of this magical island. Our classes are held on the beach at sunrise, sunset and each month, in the late evening under a beautiful full moon.

We have been practicing yoga in this way for many years and we would love for you to join us, be present and free your mind. We are a yoga club for all levels and no prior yoga experience is required.

Why Yoga?

There are many wonderful benefits to practicing yoga.

Physical

Yoga improves strength, balance and flexibility. It can ease pain and improve mobility in people with lower back pain. For those suffering with arthritis, gentle yoga has been shown to ease some of the discomfort associated.

Cardiovascular

Regular yoga practice may reduce levels of stress and body-wide inflammation, contributing to healthier hearts. Several of the factors contributing to heart disease, including high blood pressure and excess weight, can also be addressed through yoga.

Mental

Yoga relaxes you and can help you manage stress, have brighter moods and give you more energy. A bedtime yoga routine can help you get in the right mindset and prepare your body to fall asleep and stay asleep longer.

Social

Participating in yoga classes can ease loneliness and provide an environment for group healing and support. Even during one-on-one sessions loneliness is reduced as one is acknowledged as a unique individual, being listened to and participating in the creation of a personalized yoga plan.
